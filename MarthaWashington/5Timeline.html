Timeline of Martha Washington's life

  • 2 June 1731 - Martha Dandridge is born at Chestnut Grove in New Kent County, Va., to Jack Dandridge and Frances Jones Dandridge.
  • 15 May 1750 - Martha Dandridge marries planter Daniel Parke Custis at her home, Chesnut Grove, in New Kent County, Va. They move down the road to White House, the Custis plantation.
  • 19 Nov. 1751 - Martha Dandridge Custis gives birth to her first child, Daniel Parke Custis, who is named for his father.
  • April 1753 - Martha Dandridge Custis gives birth to her second child, a daughter, Frances Parke Custis.
  • Feb. 1754 - Martha Dandridge Custis’s first child, Daniel Parke Custis, falls ill with malaria and passes away shortly after his second birthday.
  • 27 Nov. 1754 - Martha Dandridge Custis has her third child and second son, John Parke Custis, nicknamed Jacky.
  • 1756 - Martha Dandridge Custis gives birth to fourth child and second daughter, Martha Parke Custis, nicknamed Patsy, her last child with her husband, Daniel Parke Custis.
  • April 1757 - Martha Dandridge Custis second child, Frances Parke Custis, dies, joining her older brother Daniel Parke Custis in the family plot at Queen’s Creek, Va.
  • 4 July 1757 - Martha Dandridge Custis son, Jacky, and her husband, Daniel Parke Custis, fall ill, possibly from a viral or parasitic infection.
  • 8 July 1757 - Martha Dandridge Custis’s husband, Daniel Parke Custis, succumbs to the infection and passes away, leaving her a very wealthy widow with two children to care for.
  • March 1758 - Martha Dandridge Custis meets George Washington for the first time. There are conflicting stories of their first meeting, but it is believed that George Washington stopped by the Widow Custis’s home on his way to Williamsburg, Va.
  • 6 Jan. 1759 - Martha Dandridge Custis and George Washington get married at White House in New Kent County, Va.
  • 19 June 1773 - Martha Dandridge Custis Washington’s daughter, Patsy, passes away suddenly at Mount Vernon after a seizure.
  • 3 Feb. 1773 - Martha Dandridge Custis Washington’s, son, Jacky, marries Eleanor Calvert, but Martha doesn't attend, as she's too distraught over her daughter's death.
  • 5 Nov. 1781 - Martha Dandridge Custis Washington’s last living child, Jacky, dies of camp fever.
  • 14 Dec. 1799 - After being ill for two days, George Washington passes away.
  • 18 Dec. 1799 - George Washington’s funeral is held, but Martha Dandridge Custis Washington is too distraught to attend.
  • 22 May 1802 - Martha Dandridge Custis Washington passes away around noon at Mount Vernon.
